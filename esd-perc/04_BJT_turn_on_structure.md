# Parasitic BJT Turn-On in ggNMOS

## Structure

NMOS cross section forms parasitic NPN:

- Emitter: Source
- Base: P-sub
- Collector: Drain

---

## Turn-On Sequence

1. High drain voltage
2. Avalanche breakdown
3. Hole current raises substrate potential
4. Base-emitter forward bias
5. NPN turns on
6. Snapback occurs

---

## Key Point

Avalanche triggers BJT conduction.
