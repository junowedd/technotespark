• “What motivated you to introduce automation instead of continuing with existing verification flows?”
    My Talking Points
    • Repeated manual tasks created bottlenecks and inconsistency.
    • Automation was necessary to scale across projects and nodes.
    • Goal was predictability, not just speed.
  • “How do you balance automation with flexibility for corner cases?”
    My Talking Points
    • Core flow automated, exceptions handled via configurable hooks.
    • Never hard-code assumptions that limit debug visibility.
    • Automation should assist engineers, not replace judgment.
  • “Can you describe a case where automation significantly changed project outcomes?”
    My Talking Points
    • Reduced verification turnaround time at late stages.
    • Improved sign-off confidence and schedule predictability.
    • Flows reused across multiple teams and programs.
