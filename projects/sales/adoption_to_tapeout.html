From Evaluation to Tape-out: Customer Success Stories

This page highlights customer success stories where early evaluation support evolved into full execution ownership through tape-out.

Engagement Flow

  • Initial technology or tool evaluation
  • Customer onboarding and flow alignment
  • Hands-on execution support during critical milestones
  • Sign-off readiness and tape-out confidence

Outcome

By maintaining continuity from evaluation to execution, customer risk was reduced and long-term trust was established.
