Featured Project: Low-Cap TVS Design Automation

Focusing on 180nm BCD Process & Signal Integrity for 10Gbps+ Interfaces

1. Depletion Region Physics

Under reverse bias, the Depletion Region acts as a voltage-dependent Dielectric. Controlling its width is key to managing junction capacitance ($C_j$).

2. Device Cross-section

A monolithic 180nm BCD structure integrating Steering Diodes for low-cap and a Vertical TVS for high-energy surge clamping.

3. Eye Diagram Integrity

Minimizing capacitance to 0.1pF is critical. Even slight parasitic increases can "close the eye," leading to fatal data errors in high-speed links.

4. Series Equivalent Model

Using a Rail-to-Rail steering topology to place capacitances in series, effectively reducing the total capacitive load on the I/O line.
