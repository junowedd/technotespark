Why Machine Learning is Critical in EUV OPC

EUV lithography introduces stochastic effects that are difficult to model using classical physics alone.

Challenges in EUV

  • Photon shot noise
  • Resist stochastic variability
  • Random defect formation
These effects are statistical, not deterministic.

Role of ML

  • Learn stochastic distributions
  • Predict defect probability
  • Guide OPC to minimize failure risk

Why Physics Alone Is Not Enough

Traditional OPC assumes deterministic imaging, which breaks down in EUV regimes.

Key Takeaway

ML is essential to manage EUV stochasticity.
