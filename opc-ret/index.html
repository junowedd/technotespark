OPC / RET Technical Notes

Production-oriented notes on OPC and RET, covering Calibre-based flows, partial ILT strategies, and ML-assisted computational lithography used in advanced nodes.

This page curates a collection of production-oriented technical notes on OPC (Optical Proximity Correction), RET (Resolution Enhancement Technology), and advanced lithography optimization.
